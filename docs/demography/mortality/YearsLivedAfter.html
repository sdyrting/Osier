YearsLivedAfter

YearsLivedAfter(MortHandle,Age)

Returns \(T_x\), the person-years lived above Age \(x\). It is given by the integral of the survival fraction from \(x\) to \(\infty\) $$ T_x = \int_{x}^{\infty} l(a) da $$
