BasisSplines

BasisSplines(XValue,XRange,Degree,[NLo],[NMid],[NHi])

Returns the value at XValue of the set of basis splines with knots given by XRange. Degree is the degree of the spline. NLo, NMid, and NHi are the number of continuity conditions at the lowest knot, interior knots, and highest knot respectively. If not specified they default to NLo=0, NMid=Degree, and NHi=0.
